University Committees Introduction#

  • Faculty Subcommittee on Academic Assessment
    • next meeting will be September 10th at 11am, online
    • These meetings are scheduled depending on the need to aprove new reports that are being developed by each department to each program NJIT offers (Graduate and PhD, and even certificates), to make sure there a "universal" assessment on those programs. All programs are being reviewed by this committee where we need to approve/approve with comments or disapprove the reports delivered by the Departments.
  • Instructional Delivery Subcommittee
    • Tuesdays at 10:30-11:30AM Fenster 398
    • This Subcommittee covers several topics: vaccination, types of instructional delivery mode, registration of students, opinions of students depending on the topic, etc.
